# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends using Power BI. The objective was to uncover key business insights, identify revenue drivers, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboard visualizations.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer segments, product performance, profitability trends, and regional sales outcomes, helping stakeholders understand business performance and areas for improvement.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales and profitability performance.
* Identify high-performing customer segments.
* Evaluate product and brand profitability.
* Assess customer trends and retention patterns.
* Compare profitability across regions.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* Data Modeling
* DAX Measures
* Data Visualization
* Dashboard Design

## Key Insights

* Medium-income customers generated the highest profit contribution, making them a highly valuable customer segment.
* Revenue fluctuated over time but showed a slight downward trend overall.
* Apple products consistently outperformed other brands in profitability.
* White-colored products were the most preferred by customers.
* Customer performance gradually declined over time, indicating potential customer retention challenges.
* The Northern region generated the highest profit, while the Western region recorded the lowest profitability.

## Key Takeaways

* Apple products are a major driver of overall profitability and business performance.
* Customer activity and performance reduced over time, which may indicate low customer retention or reduced repeat purchases.
* Regional performance varies significantly, creating opportunities for targeted improvement strategies.
* Product preferences can support inventory planning and marketing decisions.

## Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts for underperforming product brands to diversify revenue sources.
* Implement customer retention initiatives such as loyalty programs and personalized promotions.
* Develop targeted sales strategies to improve performance in the Western region.
* Prioritize inventory planning based on high-demand products and customer preferences.
* Monitor customer trends regularly to identify and address retention issues early.
